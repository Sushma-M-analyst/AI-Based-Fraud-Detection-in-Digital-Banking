# A Study on AI-Based Fraud Detection in Digital Banking Transactions

## Project Overview

The rapid growth of digital banking has increased the risk of fraudulent financial activities such as phishing, identity theft, unauthorized transactions, account takeovers, and money laundering. Traditional fraud detection systems often struggle to identify evolving fraud patterns.

This research project explores the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) techniques in detecting fraudulent banking transactions and improving digital banking security.

## Objectives

* Study the role of AI and Machine Learning in fraud detection.
* Analyze transaction patterns and suspicious activities.
* Improve fraud detection accuracy.
* Reduce false transaction alerts.
* Strengthen digital banking security.
* Explore predictive analytics and anomaly detection techniques.

## Key Findings

* AI-based fraud detection systems provide faster and more accurate fraud identification.
* Machine Learning models can detect hidden transaction patterns.
* Real-time monitoring improves banking security.
* AI helps reduce financial losses and false alerts.
* Intelligent fraud detection enhances customer trust.
